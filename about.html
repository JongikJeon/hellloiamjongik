Ideator & Maker
Jongik Jeon is an ideator and maker, delving into the fields of tangible interface, novel interaction and auditory experience. He specializes in harnessing new technologies and transforming futuristic ideas into real-world applications.

Jongik believes that emerging technologies will introduce novel interactions and experiences across various aspects of our future lives. His goal is to innovate people's future experience through a design-based approach.

Jongik is currently studying Industrial Design and Culture Technology at KAIST.
